Reinventing the wheel

To design is to ask questions

"What's different about it?"
If we're doing the exact same thing, what are we even doing?

"What's unnecesary?"
If it adds nothing, then it shouldn't be there

"Is it perfect?"
Nothing ever is; you have to keep looking ever forward

"What does it mean?"
Art that makes you feel nothing, stands for nothing, and says nothing, isn't art: it's trash

These, and many like it, are the questions that lay at the heart of my work

999 Blocks

A musical, emergent puzzle about exploring possibilities! Move and arrange blocks in certain patterns to create different blocks, which can create other patterns that lead to more blocks! The vast possibility space constantly creates opportunities for emergent gameplay that invite you to experiment and find new ways to organise the production of blocks… or revel in the chaos!

Roles: Designer and programmer

Beyond the Gate

The Horrors on the other side are trying to open a Gate. If they do, it's the end. You must finish an occult ritual to close the portal before they can cross over, but time is limited - perhaps an offering of flesh may distract them for long enough...

A horror game about frantically trying to puzzle out and assemble a magic circle before it's too late - the portal grows bigger by the second, and the Horrors grow ever more ravenous...

Roles: Designer and programmer

VideoItchRead more

Las sensaciones clave a emitir con este juego eran el horror ante lo que se avecina y la urgencia de tener que resolver un problema a toda velocidad antes de que todo se vaya al garete. El juego en sí es un puzzle extremadamente simple, siendo lo interesante que tienes que investigar activamente exactamente qué es lo que hay que hacer y por qué. Beyond the Gate is a game about foreboding horror: there is a very big problem to solve, with a lot of unknown information, and you have to puzzle it out fast before everything ends. The puzzle itself is very simple: the difficulty lies within the player themself, having to puzzle out an answer under a time pressure that is not spelled out by mechanics, but rather, text - like a real world problem.

The Apocalypse Of You

The apocalypse came and went, and now only silence remains. You may survive starvation or the things crawling in the dark, but the true struggle is keeping hope alive.

The Apocalypse of You is a game about the human element in a hopeless situation. You'll have to deal with loneliness, anxiety and paranoia as you try to move on in your life as the last remaining person in the world.
